Hassle

A gentle planner for hard days.

Hassle helps people living with chronic illness, chronic pain, fatigue, and neurodivergence plan around the energy they actually have.

Privacy Policy Terms of Service

What Hassle does

  • Track energy in spoons or battery %
  • Plan tasks around real capacity
  • Mark flare days and reflect gently
  • Notice patterns over time

Privacy by default

  • No ads
  • No analytics or tracking
  • Works locally without an account
  • Optional sync only if you sign in
