Hassle — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

Hassle ("the app", "we") is a daily planner for people living with chronic illness and neurodivergence. We built it to be private by default. This policy explains what we collect — and mostly don't.

The short version

  • The app works fully on your device with no account. By default, your data never leaves your phone.
  • We show no ads and use no third-party analytics or tracking.
  • Syncing is optional. Only if you sign in does your data leave the device, and then only to your own private account.

What you enter

Hassle stores what you choose to log: energy levels, tasks, flare days, symptom and reflection notes, an optional name, and any conditions you tag. Some of this is health-related information, which we treat as sensitive and never use for advertising or profiling.

Where it's stored, and who processes it

  • On your device (default): saved locally. Uninstalling removes it.
  • In your account (only if you enable sync): mirrored to our backend provider, Supabase, encrypted in transit and at rest. Row-level security means only your signed-in account can read your data.

Accounts & sign-in

Sign-in is optional, via Sign in with Apple or Google. If you sign in we receive a user identifier and, unless you use Apple's Hide My Email, an email address, used only to operate your account and sync. Payments for Hassle Plus are processed by Apple or Google — we never receive your card details.

Legal basis (UK/EU GDPR)

Where GDPR applies, we rely on your consent for optional sync of health-related data, which you can withdraw at any time by signing out, and our legitimate interests in operating and securing the app.

What we don't do

No advertising or ad identifiers. No analytics SDKs or behavioural tracking. No data brokers. We never sell your data.

Your rights & control

  • Use the app with no account at all.
  • Clear all app data at any time in Settings.
  • Stop syncing by signing out; your local data stays on device.
  • Access, correct, export, or delete your data by using the app controls or emailing us.

Retention

Local data stays until you delete it or uninstall. Synced data is kept while your account is active and deleted on request or account deletion.

Children

Hassle is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 where applicable) and we do not knowingly collect their data.

Changes

We'll update this page and the date above when this policy changes.
