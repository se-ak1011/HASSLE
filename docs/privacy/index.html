Hassle — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 July 2026

Hassle ("the app", "we", "us") is a gentle daily companion for people living with chronic illness, chronic pain, fatigue, and neurodivergence. We built it to be private by default. This policy explains what we collect — and mostly don't — and your rights under UK and EU data protection law (UK GDPR and EU GDPR).

Who is responsible for your data

The data controller is the developer of Hassle, contactable at drainedstore@gmail.com. Use that address for any privacy request or question.

The short version

  • The app works fully on your device with no account. By default, your data never leaves your phone.
  • We show no ads and use no third-party analytics or tracking.
  • Syncing and AI features are optional. Your data only leaves the device if you turn sync on or use an AI feature.
  • You can export, clear, or permanently delete everything at any time.

What you enter

Hassle stores what you choose to log: energy levels, tasks, flare days, symptoms, medication notes, reflections and journals, letters to yourself, your garden progress, an optional name, and any conditions you tag. Some of this is health-related information — a "special category" of data under GDPR — which we treat as sensitive, and never use for advertising or profiling.

Where it's stored, and who processes it

  • On your device (default): saved locally. Clearing app data or uninstalling removes it.
  • In your account (only if you enable sync): mirrored to our backend provider, Supabase, encrypted in transit and at rest. Row-level security means only your signed-in account can read your data.
  • AI features (only when you use them): when you talk to Lola or generate a summary or doctor report, the text you send is processed by our AI provider, Anthropic, solely to generate that response. It is not used to train models.

Accounts & sign-in

Sign-in is optional, via Sign in with Apple or Google. If you sign in we receive a user identifier and, unless you use Apple's Hide My Email, an email address — used only to operate your account and sync your data. We never receive payment or card details.

Notifications

If you turn on reminders or seal a "letter to yourself", the app schedules local notifications on your device. These are optional and controlled entirely by you.

Legal bases (UK/EU GDPR)

  • Explicit consent — for syncing health-related (special category) data to your account, and for sending text to our AI provider when you use an AI feature. You can withdraw consent at any time by turning off sync / not using AI, or by deleting your data.
  • Performance of a contract — to provide the account and sync you asked for.
  • Legitimate interests — to operate, secure, and debug the app, balanced against your rights.

International transfers

Our processors (Supabase and Anthropic) may store or process data on servers outside the UK/EEA, including in the United States. Where they do, those transfers are covered by appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses under the processors' terms.

What we don't do

No advertising or ad identifiers. No analytics SDKs or behavioural tracking. No data brokers. We never sell your data.

Your rights

Under UK/EU GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access a copy of your data.
  • Rectify data that's wrong.
  • Erase your data ("right to be forgotten") — delete your account and all synced data from Settings › Account, or clear all on-device data from Settings.
  • Restrict or object to processing.
  • Portability — export your data from within the app.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.

To exercise any right, use the in-app controls or email drainedstore@gmail.com. You also have the right to complain to a supervisory authority — in the UK, the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk, or your local authority in the EU.

Retention

Local data stays until you clear it or uninstall. Synced data is kept while your account is active and is deleted when you delete your account or on request. AI request text is processed transiently to produce a response and is not retained by us.

Children

Hassle is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 where applicable) and we do not knowingly collect their data.

Health & safety

Hassle is a self-management companion — not a medical device, a diagnosis, or a substitute for professional care. Nothing in the app, including anything Lola says, is medical advice. If you're in crisis or think you may be in danger, contact your local emergency services or a crisis line right away.
