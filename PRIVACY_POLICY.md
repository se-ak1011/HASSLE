Hassle ("the app", "we") is a daily planner for people living with chronic
illness and neurodivergence, operated by [YOUR NAME / COMPANY], [LOCATION]. We
built it to be private by default. This policy explains what we collect — and
mostly don't.

## The short version
- The app works **fully on your device with no account**. By default, your data
  never leaves your phone.
- We show **no ads** and use **no third-party analytics or tracking**.
- **Syncing is optional.** Only if you sign in does your data leave the device,
  and then only to your own private account.

## What you enter
Hassle stores what you choose to log: energy levels, tasks, flare days, symptom
and reflection notes, an optional name, and any conditions you tag. Some of this
is **health-related information**, which we treat as a special, sensitive
category and never use for advertising or profiling.

## Where it's stored, and who processes it
- **On your device (default):** saved locally. Uninstalling removes it.
- **In your account (only if you enable sync):** mirrored to our backend
  provider, **Supabase** (our processor), encrypted in transit and at rest, in
  the **[REGION, e.g. EU (Frankfurt)]** region. Row-level security means **only
  your signed-in account can read your data.**

## Accounts & sign-in
Sign-in is optional, via **Sign in with Apple** or **Google**. If you sign in we
receive a user identifier and (unless you use Apple's Hide My Email) an email
address, used only to operate your account and sync. Payments for Hassle Plus are
processed by Apple/Google — we never receive your card details.

## Legal basis (UK/EU GDPR)
Where GDPR applies, we rely on: your **consent** (for optional sync of
health-related data, which you can withdraw at any time by signing out) and our
**legitimate interests** in operating and securing the app.

## What we DON'T do
No advertising or ad identifiers. No analytics SDKs or behavioural tracking. No
data brokers. We never sell your data.

## Your rights & control
- Use the app with **no account** at all.
- **Clear all app data** any time in Settings (removes local data).
- **Stop syncing** by signing out (your local data stays).
- **Access, correct, export, or delete** your data: most is in your control in
  the app; for synced data, sign in and clear data, or email us and we will
  delete your account data. You may also complain to your local data protection
  authority (in the UK, the ICO).

## Retention
Local data stays until you delete it or uninstall. Synced data is kept while your
account is active and deleted on request / account deletion.

## Children
Hassle is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 where applicable) and we
do not knowingly collect their data.

We'll update this page and the "last updated" date when this policy changes.
