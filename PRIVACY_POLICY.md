Hassle ("the app", "we") is a daily planner for people living with chronic
illness and neurodivergence. We built it to be private by default. This policy
explains what we do — and mostly don't — collect.

## The short version
- The app works **fully on your device with no account**. By default, your data
  never leaves your phone.
- We show **no ads** and use **no third-party analytics or tracking** of any kind.
- **Syncing is optional.** Only if you choose to sign in does your data leave the
  device, and then only to your own private account so you can back it up and use
  it on more than one device.

## What you enter
Hassle stores what you choose to log: energy levels, tasks, flare days, symptom
and reflection notes, optional name, and any conditions you tag. Some of this is
**health-related information**, which we treat as sensitive.

## Where it's stored
- **On your device** (default): saved locally on your phone. Uninstalling the app
  removes it.
- **In your account** (only if you opt into sync): mirrored to our backend
  provider, **Supabase**, in encrypted transit and at rest. Access is protected
  by row-level security, so **only your signed-in account can read your data**.

## Accounts & sign-in
Sign-in is optional and handled by **Sign in with Apple** or **Google**. If you
sign in, we receive a user identifier and (unless you use Apple's Hide My Email)
an email address, used solely to operate your account and sync. We do not sell or
share this with anyone.

## What we DON'T do
- No advertising, no ad identifiers.
- No analytics SDKs, no behavioural tracking, no data brokers.
- We never sell your data.

## Hassle Plus
Optional paid features are billed by Apple (App Store) / Google. We don't receive
your card details — payment is handled by the platform.

## Your control
- Use the app with no account at all.
- **Clear all app data** any time in Settings.
- Stop syncing by signing out (your local data stays).
- To delete account-synced data, sign in and clear data, or contact us at the
  email below and we will delete it.

## Children
Hassle is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 where applicable) and we
do not knowingly collect their data.
