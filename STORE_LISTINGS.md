## Description (≤4000)
Hassle is a daily planner for bodies that don't always cooperate.

Built for people living with chronic illness, chronic pain, fatigue, and
neurodivergence (ADHD, autism, AuDHD), Hassle helps you plan around the energy
you actually have — not the energy you're "supposed" to.

No productivity pressure. No guilt. No streaks to break. Just an honest, gentle
way to get through the day.

WHAT YOU CAN DO
• Track your energy in spoons or battery % — however you think about it
• Add tasks and see what they truly "cost" before you overcommit
• Move a task to another day, without shame, when today isn't the day
• Mark flare days so your plan eases off with you
• Reflect with short, optional prompts
• Notice patterns over time — energy, flares, and what tends to drain you

ALWAYS FREE
Every part of the core app is free, forever — tracking, tasks, flares,
reflection, patterns, and a 7-day PDF summary.

HASSLE PLUS (optional)
A small subscription that helps fund development and adds a few extras:
• A reading library of plain-language articles, each linked to a real study
• Doctor-visit reports — a clinical PDF of your trends and notes for appointments
• A specialist directory (coming soon)

PRIVATE BY DEFAULT
Your data lives on your device. No ads. No tracking. No analytics. Optional sync
is there only if you want your data across devices.

Hassle is an information and planning tool — not medical advice. Please talk to
your clinician about your health.

Made with care for the spoonie and neurodivergent community. 💜
